How We Started

Founded on a Simple Complaint

Prime Estates began in 2009 when our founder, tired of opaque pricing and vanishing agents, decided to build the brokerage he wished existed — one where every listing is verified in person and every client gets a single point of contact from first viewing to final registration.

What started as a two-person office near Indiranagar now operates across Bengaluru's fastest-growing corridors, with a team that has collectively closed over a thousand transactions.
